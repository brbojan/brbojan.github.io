photography

Photographs captured in different locations around the globe. Gallery includes various styles - landscape, urban and documentary.

Fine-art photography is photography created in line with the vision of the photographer as artist, using photography as a medium for creative expression. The goal is to express an idea, a message, or an emotion. It is essentially an art form practiced for its beauty. It’s all about why it was created and comes from a concept or idea derived from the artist. It can also provide documentary visual account of specific subjects and events.
The purpose of fine art is to simply exist.

graphic design

Graphic gallery highlighting work which is mostly used in commercial purposes. Logo, bussiness card, web banner design and more.

Graphic design is the process of visual communication and problem-solving through the use of typography, photography, and illustration. The field is considered a subset of visual communication and communication design, but sometimes the term "graphic design" is used synonymously. Graphic designers create and combine symbols, images and text to form visual representations of ideas and messages. They use typography, visual arts, and page layout techniques to create visual compositions.

three-dimensional design

Collection of three-dimensional work of different sorts. From simple sketches and illustrations to renders and visualization of projects.

In 3D computer graphics, 3D modeling is the process of developing a mathematical representation of any surface of an object (either inanimate or living) in three dimensions via specialized software. The product is called a 3D model. Someone who works with 3D models may be referred to as a 3D artist. It can be displayed as a two-dimensional image through a process called 3D rendering or used in a computer simulation of physical phenomena. The model can also be physically created using 3D printing devices.
